Your Guide Through Every Step

For more than three decades, I've helped families navigate some of life's biggest transitions. My background in healthcare, senior living, and real estate gives me a unique understanding of the challenges families face when helping aging parents.

I created the Next Chapter Made Easy™ Blueprint because families deserve more than a real estate transaction. They deserve a trusted guide — someone who understands the emotional side of letting go of a family home, who understands senior care options, and who can help simplify the process and bring clarity during a difficult time.

"My goal isn't just to help sell a home. It's to help families feel supported, informed, and confident every step of the way."

Families throughout Charlotte, Lake Norman, and the surrounding communities turn to me for guidance because I bring together senior transition planning, healthcare knowledge, and real estate expertise in one place.
